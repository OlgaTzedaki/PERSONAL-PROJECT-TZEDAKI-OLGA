DECEMBER 2022 ISSUE

Happy Holidays!

Given that these are approaching the new year, I found it fitting to take some time to reflect on what has happened and note down some of the things I'm grateful for. I genuinely believe that kickstarting 2023 feeling content and aware of one's blessings is the ideal way of ensuring that 2023 will be just as good, if not better!
Reminder: It is the small wins that make a big difference :)

I believe that, generally speaking, 2022 was a hard year on all. The ongoing war in Ukraine will forever remain one of the darkest moments in the history of the 21st century, and I'd like to express my sincere condolences to the victims' families. Apart from that, our inflation - stricken fellow citizens are facing difficulties making ends meet. I do not want to appear shallow by stating that 2022 has been good to me, but it is the truth. So, forever keeping in mind those who have not been treated well by this year, I'd like to list some of the moments that made 2022 a relatively good year. I think happiness is found in the little things; I strongly suggest you too note down what brought you joy in the past year. Who knows, it might well be the secret recipe for happiness in 2023, although change and development are always welcome!

  • When I got my dream phone as a Christmas surprise-present.
  • When I participated in my first greek debate event and was ranked 13th best speaker.
  • The startling amount of snow I was met with when waking up on a Saturday morning.
  • When I visited an adventure park in March, while it'd snowed!
  • When I visited the centre of Athens with my friends after a really long time.
  • When my friends surpised me with a cake on my birthday.
  • When me and my friends visited my favorite restaurant for my birthday and ate some yummy food!
  • When, a week after my birthday, we went on a 4 - day trip to Syros island with my class.
  • The fact that I managed to not let my body image struggles dominate my life for a little while.
  • That my efforts came to fruition and I know attend the school of my dreams.
  • When one of my closest friends and me went out for sushi and crêpes to celebrate the new year.

The final verdict is that April 2022 must have been the best month of the year XD
